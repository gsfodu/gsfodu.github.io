About Us

Global Student Friendship (GSF) is a student organization that was established in 2005. Driven by the principles of faith, GSF is supported by international students, American families, and faculty members. The organization's primary aim is to assist scholars and students from diverse regions and cultures across the globe.

Mission and Vision

Mission

At Global Student Friendship, we transform strangers into companions and even family by meeting their practical necessities and demonstrating unwavering love, regardless of their circumstances. We are dedicated to providing care and support to international students, scholars, and their families through the guiding force of God's love.

We hold fundamental principles guiding our organization's values and vision. We believe in the power of love, respect, and honor, treating every individual with care and consideration. Our goal is to foster an inclusive and hospitable environment that welcomes people from all backgrounds and beliefs. At GSF, we uphold integrity, honesty, and justice in all our interactions, setting high standards for ourselves and our community. We strive to serve others through kindness and compassion, promoting peace within our community and beyond.
