Activities and Events for International Students

Global Student Friendship actively engages in various activities and events tailored to cater to the needs of international students. Some of the initiatives and collaborations include:

Cultural Exchange Events

GSF organizes cultural exchange events that celebrate the diversity of cultures in the student community. These events allow students to share their traditions, customs, and cuisines, fostering a deeper appreciation for different cultures.

Support Services

Understanding the challenges of adjusting to a new environment, GSF conducts activities and provides support services to help international students adapt to life abroad successfully. These resources are provided by volunteer domestic families and student leaders who serve in our ministries: Furniture Ministry, Hospitality Ministry, Bible Study, Communication, Exchange Student Connect, Outreach, and Special Events. Furniture Ministry, Hospitality Ministry, Bible Study, Communication, Exchange Student Connect, Outreach, and Special Events.

Community Outreach

GSF engages in community outreach programs to give back to the local and global communities. Through volunteer efforts and charity initiatives, GSF aims to impact and promote goodwill positively.

Partnership with Global Friendship Ventures

As part of its commitment to assisting international students, GSF collaborates with Global Friendship Ventures, a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting local churches and charities. This partnership supports international students in the Hampton Roads region and beyond.

General Events

Tuesday Luncheon

Global Student Friendship hosts a delightful luncheon for international students every Tuesday. This event allows students to come together, share stories, and forge meaningful connections over a delicious meal. The Tuesday Luncheon satisfies hunger and nourishes the soul, as students find comfort in the company of like-minded individuals worldwide.

Friday F.I.S.H

Global Student Friendship invites all international students to partake in the Friday F.I.S.H (Friday International Supper and Hospitality) event on Friday evenings. This special gathering aims to create a warm and welcoming space for students to unwind and socialize after a week of academic endeavors. The event features an array of international cuisines, providing a delightful culinary journey that represents the diverse backgrounds of the attendees.
