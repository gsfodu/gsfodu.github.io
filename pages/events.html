GSF Event Calendar

General Events

Tuesday Luncheon

Global Student Friendship hosts a delightful luncheon for international students every Tuesday. This event allows students to come together, share stories, and forge meaningful connections over a delicious meal. The Tuesday Luncheon satisfies hunger and nourishes the soul, as students find comfort in the company of like-minded individuals worldwide.

Friday F.I.S.H

Global Student Friendship invites all international students to partake in the Friday F.I.S.H (Friday International Supper and Hospitality) event on Friday evenings. This special gathering aims to create a warm and welcoming space for students to unwind and socialize after a week of academic endeavors. The event features an array of international cuisines, providing a delightful culinary journey that represents the diverse backgrounds of the attendees.
