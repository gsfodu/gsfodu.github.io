Exchange Students Team

The Ministry focuses on supporting exchange students who may be new to the ODU or Norfolk region and want to become more familiar with their surroundings. These students often arrive in the US without any existing social networks, which can lead to feelings of loneliness and isolation. To address this, the Ministry offers a range of activities and events that enable these students to explore the campus and the broader community, meet new people, and create memorable experiences. Through these initiatives, the Ministry aims to help exchange students adjust to their new environment and feel more comfortable and connected during their time at ODU.
