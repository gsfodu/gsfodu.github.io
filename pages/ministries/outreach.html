Outreach Team

The outreach team is responsible for helping international students with practical needs beyond just furniture and food services. They also participate in university activities on campus and collaborate with local charitable organizations to carry out various outreach projects in the community. The outreach team works closely with these organizations, coordinating their efforts to recruit student volunteers, conduct outreach events, and hold meetings with university or charitable organizations. To effectively carry out their services, the outreach team members not only work hard and plan events but also exhibit a compassionate and caring spirit towards those they serve.
