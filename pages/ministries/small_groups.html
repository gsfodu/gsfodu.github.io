Small Groups

The organization provides a range of resources and support related to Bible studies, including the formation of Bible study groups for individuals who wish to engage in group discussions and teachings centered around the Bible. Additionally, the organization offers various forms of support, such as providing materials to facilitate the study of the Bible, and offering training sessions for Bible study leaders to enhance their skills and knowledge in leading Bible studies. Through these initiatives, the organization seeks to promote the understanding and application of biblical teachings among individuals and communities, and to encourage personal and spiritual growth through the study of the Bible.
